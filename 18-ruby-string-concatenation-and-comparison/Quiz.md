## Concatenating String in Ruby

จงบอกผลลัพธ์ที่ได้จากโปรแกรมนี้
```
a = "When is your " 
b = "daughter's "
c = "graduation "
d = "ceremony?"

puts a.concat(b).concat(c)+d
puts a

```
<details close>
<summary><b>output</b></summary>
  <pre> 
    When is your daughter's graduation ceremony?
    When is your daughter's graduation 
 </pre>
</details>
